void liquid::DeltaTime::tick ( )

Simulates a tick of the DeltaTime (in milliseconds)

Will simulate a tick of DeltaTime by taing the current time using the mClock variable and taking away the last tick time. Then if the last X amount of frames as defined by DELTA_CACHE_SIZE have become far between a correction will be attempted by averaging the delta over the cache.
