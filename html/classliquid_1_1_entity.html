◆ update()

void liquid::Entity::update ( )
virtual

Update every frame.

Intended to be called when the game updates, it is useful for moving the Entity or computing something that is important to its running. Usually called around every 1/60th of a second.

If you need to get the delta time to compute something without relying on the steps of frames look at the DeltaTime class in Utilities.
